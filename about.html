About Herty Afari

Herty Afari is a passionate content creator, speaker, and mentor who believes in the power of real conversations and authentic connection. As the creator and host of The Pink Chronicles, Herty started this podcast to inspire, uplift, and empower girls and women to embrace their true selves and chase their dreams without apology.

Her mission is simple:

To create a world where every girl knows her voice matters and her story has power.
